Who are we?

We are a group of developers and traders interested in solving technical problems

Tulip solutions was founded by developers and traders that dealt with the quirks of exchanges. They believe cryptocurrencies, and especially Bitcoin, will not only be here to stay, but will become a large part of the financial market. However, they realized the surrounding infrastructure is not yet on par with what we are used to in the financial sector. So, as a first step they set out to help exchanges improve their platforms by redesigning and rebuilding exchange infrastructure.

More than five years algorithmic trading experience

We've actively traded for the last 5 years on the majority of cryptocurrency exchanges.

Interested in solving technical problems

We're a bunch of developers with more than 10 years of experience, interested in solving technical challenges.

More than 10 years linux sysadmin experience

We have been administrating Linux systems for over 10 years.

More than five years of IT security experience

We are IT security professionals with over five years of experience in high assurance environments.

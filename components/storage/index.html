Storage engine

Prevent your customers from missing any historic trade, order or transaction data with the Tulip Solutions storage engine.

Storing historical order and trade information becomes expensive and difficult to manage. Trading data is requested by various actors which require different views and it can accumulate to millions of entries per user over time. You may have started with a simple database, but can no longer serve customers with data they request while paying a ton of hosting fees to your cloud providers. Our storage solution tackles this by providing your users instant access to their data at a very low total cost of ownership for you.

Cost efficient storage

Our storage solution minimizes the storage footprint and costs by making use of an optimized data layout for trading data.

Straightforward query options

Query order events (trades, order creation, cancels) by an individual user, market and choose whether to query back or forward in time. See Get order events

Lightning fast queries

Due to our unique storage layout, you can serve large bulks of historic user data without suffering connection timeouts.

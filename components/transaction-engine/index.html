Payment processing Engine

Connects incoming and outgoing transactions with the matching engine and can integrate with any other required services.

Our transaction engine takes care of consistency between transactions (withdraws and deposits) and user balances. It tracks the status of the transactions cryptocurrency/bank accounts and notifies the matching engine when a transaction is confirmed. The engine is modular and extendable allowing you to choose which service should sign, analyze and broadcast the transaction.
